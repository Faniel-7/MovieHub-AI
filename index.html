The Amazing Spider-Man follows Peter Parker, a high school student who gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a genetically altered spider. As he struggles to balance his normal life with his new powers, he also searches for the truth about his parents’ disappearance. When a dangerous scientist transforms into Lizard, Peter must step up as Spider-Man to protect the city, learning responsibility, courage, and the cost of being a hero.
